So what's this all about?

I am Sophia, For those who don't know me- I’m a wife, a mother of six grown children and many grandchildren. I was born into as large family in Russia, where my parents kept fighting to keep our Jewish faith alive in Communist Era. Eventually , we were able to escape to Israel where I got married and moved to the USA. In my early years I was a teacher and educator at different Jewish schools . Throughout my teaching career , I noticed how we put so much emphasis on our Faith and studies, and often neglect to take care of our health and nutrition . I also noticed health issues arising from weight gain and health disorders , I decided to start researching and hearing different natural ways, that I can make an impact on my community. I took him home ... against medical advice ..and started feeding him the micronutrients. And only after three days he went from non-responsive to moving his head and arm- I saw light at the end of the tunnel ! Long story short- he was eating solid food within three weeks! My Goals, My goal is simple and bold ! Together with my team - I want to help change the nutritional habits and impact my community to live healthy , active lifestyle. It’s my passion and commitment, to help everyone I can to get healthier and I go beyond the expectations to get the job done. Continue Reading
